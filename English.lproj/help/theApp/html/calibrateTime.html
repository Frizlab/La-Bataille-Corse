Reaction time calibration

Warning : concentrate before you begin the calibration process !

Cards are put on the desk at a regular pace, but adapting to your previous reactions.
You must bang according to the rules of the game.

A wrong bang clears the pile, but is not taken into account: the top card flashes to show the mistake.

The calibration terminates after 15 righteous bangs: it's a bit long, but proved necessary to obtain a significant value.

You can stop the calibration process by closing the window.
