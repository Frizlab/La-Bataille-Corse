How to play the game

Each player plays a card in turn, until a court card is played (Jolly, Queen, King or Ace) or someone bangs.
The reasons to bang are detailed in When to bang ?

When a player bangs

  • If he's right, he wins the pile.
  • If he's wrong, he puts a card beneath the pile (in certain variants: several cards, to be decided at the beginning of the game).

When a court card is played

The next player must put a certain number of cards in a row:
  • One for a Jolly
  • Two for a Queen
  • Three for a King
  • Four for an Ace

If a court card is put during this process, it is interrupted. The next player initiates an other one, and must put the number of cards corresponding to this last court card.

Bang conditions always take precedence, and also interrupt the process: when you have to bang, do it !

If the player puts all the cards he had to without being interrupted, the pile goes to the player who played the court card.

When a player has no card left, the next player puts the card (or cards) he should have put.

The End of the Game

The game is over when one of the players handles all 54 cards of the deck.
This means that the game continues even if only one player still has some cards in hand: he is alone to play the cards, but the other players are still allowed to bang and win the pile.

Good luck !
