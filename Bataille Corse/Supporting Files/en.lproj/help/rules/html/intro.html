Introduction

The Bataille Corse is more a fast speed game than a deep reflexion game.
But many variants involve the brains for simple yet very quick deductions, making it a really addictive game.

Roughly speaking, each player plays a card in turn, and any player must bang the pile of cards when certain patterns occur. The fastest wins the pile.

The number of players may vary from 2 to 54, and all players play until the end of the game, even when they have no card left.
